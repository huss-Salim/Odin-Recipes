Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, cheese, and b√©chamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Spices (oregano, basil)

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with onion and garlic.
  2. Add tomato sauce and spices, simmer.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan in a baking dish.
  5. Bake until bubbly and golden brown.
